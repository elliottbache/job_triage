We are seeking a **CFD Engineer** to support the simulation and analysis of internal flow and heat transfer systems. In this role, you will build and validate CFD models, analyze results, and support key engineering decisions.

* **Experience:** 3+ years in CFD, thermal-fluid simulation, or related engineering analysis.
* **Technical Skills:** Strong experience with **ANSYS Fluent** or **OpenFOAM** is required.
* **Automation:** Proficiency in **Python scripting** for preprocessing, postprocessing, and workflow automation.
* **Domain Knowledge:** Solid understanding of turbulence modeling, meshing, heat transfer, and Linux-based simulation environments.
* **Pluses:** Experience with **C++** is a plus.
