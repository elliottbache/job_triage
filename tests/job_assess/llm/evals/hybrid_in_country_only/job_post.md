### About Skydance Animation
Skydance offers a dynamic, inclusive, and ever-evolving culture where innovative ideas are welcomed and growth is fostered. We are specifically looking for pioneers who seek to push the limits of what the world’s most collaborative art form can achieve.

### Role Overview
As a Senior Animator, you will be responsible for giving credibility and transmitting specific emotions in each moment of the film. You must handle different styles of animation at the highest artistic level while serving as an example to the team in body mechanics, acting, facial performance, and final polish.

### Responsibilities
* Create animations at the highest artistic and technical level.
* Collaborate with the Director, Supervisor, and Leads to propose unique acting or movement aligned with the story.
* Manage assignments to reach deadlines while maintaining character models and production designs.
* Provide support, guidance, and instruction to the team to contribute to their professional growth.
* Stay current on artistic methodologies, technology trends, and tools.

### Requirements
* **Industry Experience:** 5+ years in VFX or animation industries (with at least 3+ years specifically in animation).
* **Technical Skills:** Strong artistic and/or technical aptitude in 3D animation is a must.
* **Desirables:** Drawing skills, high English proficiency, and Spanish language skills.
